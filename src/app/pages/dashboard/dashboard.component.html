How it Works

Upload Data

You are building machine learning models for your organisation and you are unsure how trustworthy they are? Use our platform to get answers to your questions. You upload your model, training data and factsheet to our platform.

Analyze

We analyze your model regarding fairness, explainablity, robustness and accountability using industry standard metrics. This will tell you how well your model performs.

Improve

Your model will be checked in many different dimension. This gives you valuable performance insights. Use this assessment in order to further enhance your model.

What Metrics Do We Apply?

Fairness

  • Underfitting
  • Overfitting
  • Statistical Parity Difference
  • Equal Opportunity Difference
  • Average Odds Difference
  • Disparate Impact
  • Class Balance

Explainability

  • Algorithm Class
  • Correlated Features
  • Model Size
  • Feature Relevance

Robustness

  • Confidence Score
  • Clique Method
  • Loss Sensitivity
  • Clever Score
  • Er Fast Gradient Attack
  • Er Carlini Wagner Attack
  • Er Deepfool Attack

Accountability

  • Normalization
  • Missing Data
  • Regularization
  • Train Test Split
  • Factsheet Completeness
